Matt Schmidt Outdoors

A legacy of of community and passion for the great outdoors.

Mission

We are driven by the memory of our friend, husband, father, son and brother Matt Schmidt. Matt was generous in every way and that example serves to fuel our passion. We have taken time to reflect on what legacy Matt would have wanted to leave behind and we think that the work we do in his name honors his actions in life and his memory. We are happy to offer various opportunities to help kids get outside, which highlights several of Matt's passions. We have established mentorship opportunities for kids in both hunting and fishing. We are also dedicated to supporting those organizations which were important to Matt and continue to be important to his children. Lastly we support a scholarship in his name as well.

Vision

We work to build positive experiences that are mutually beneficial to all participants. Please consider supporting our events as they are what allows us to continue to provide unique and meaningful opportunities for young people.
